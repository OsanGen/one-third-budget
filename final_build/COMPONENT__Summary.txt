# COMPONENT__Summary

## Purpose
Show at-a-glance monthly budget status so a first-time user can see if they are safe, close, or over limit in one glance.

## Inputs
- Revenue text parsed in page state.
- Bucket list from local reducer (`bucketState.buckets`).
- Derived values from pure helpers in `lib/money.ts`.

## Outputs
- Section title `Summary`.
- Primary summary line with one of:
  - `Still safe to spend` (under cap)
  - `0 left` (exactly at cap)
  - `Over the cap by` (over cap)
- Live, polite announcements for key summary state changes through a hidden status region:
  - no revenue
  - at cap
  - over cap
  - under cap
- Quiet support text on over-cap, like:
  - `Fastest way back under: DoorDash, Fun Activities, Gaming`
- Legend row list for each bucket with:
  - small color dot
  - bucket name
  - dollar amount
  - percent of cap
- Primary number that matches the line:
  - remaining amount, `$0.00`, or overage.
- Status pill with one of `Safe`, `Close to cap`, `Over cap`.
- Supporting visible lines for:
  - total in buckets
  - bucket cap
  - used so far
- Progress bar area showing fill and cap usage text.
- Donut ring chart in a calm side area of the section to reinforce current usage.

## State and derived logic
- `bucketTotal = getBucketTotal(bucketState.buckets)`
- `bucketCap = getCap(monthlyRevenue)`
- `bucketStatus = getStatus(bucketCap, bucketTotal)`
- `remaining = getRemaining(bucketCap, bucketTotal)`
- `usagePercent = getUsagePercent(bucketCap, bucketTotal)`
- `progressPercent = clamp(usagePercent, 0, 100)`
- `overage = bucketTotal - bucketCap` when `bucketStatus` is `over`
- `highlightedBucketId` selected from legend taps/clicks or row focus

## Progress behavior
- Fill is based on `usagePercent` and rendered as a calm rounded bar with INK outline.
- Progress track itself is intentionally quiet and non-assertive to reduce repeated announcements; major numbers and status are communicated by text and status region.
- Status text is always visible in the section with the pill so no user depends on color.
- At 100% or more the bar is visually capped at full track.
- Over-cap state is shown as a full bar plus explicit overage text: `$X.XX over` and `Usage is over by ...`.
- Empty/zero revenue keeps bar visible at 0% with non-alarming helper copy.
- Donut tone mapping:
  - neutral/setup: blue
  - safe: mint
  - close: yellow
  - over: pink
- Donut center label:
  - under cap: `$X.00 left`
  - at cap: `$0.00 left`
  - over cap: `$X.00 over`
- No-bucket state:
  - ring remains empty.
  - supportive helper text appears: add a bucket and amount to make the ring meaningful.
- Interaction:
  - clicking/tapping a legend row sets `highlightedBucketId`
  - keyboard focus on a legend row sets the same highlight
  - matching bucket card receives `bucket-card-highlighted` style
  - matching chart segment increases stroke emphasis
- No separate color-only meaning; text labels and structure stay visible during highlight.
- Scene accent is restrained to one tone per state, with panel/legend emphasis inheriting from that tone.

## Edge cases
- Revenue blank or zero:
  - summary still renders and tells user to add monthly money in.
- Exact-at-cap condition:
  - shows `0 left` and matching copy.
- Over-cap:
  - shows overage and a calm adjustment suggestion.
  - the suggestion uses up to three largest buckets from current totals.
- Non-numeric or blank bucket amounts:
  - all numbers come from `parseMoneyInput`-safe values via `getBucketTotal`.

## Acceptance checks
- The bar reads quickly on phone with low visual noise.
- `used` copy and bar fill agree on the same numbers.
- Quiet accents and heading separators support scanability without drawing attention from primary numbers.
- Over-cap is clear and calm without alarm tone.
- Empty states are understandable and actionable.
