# COMPONENT__HeaderStrip

## Purpose
Give first-time users immediate clarity at the top of the page without making the layout busy.

## Inputs
- Static title: `One Third Budget`
- Static one-line purpose sentence
- Static reassurance sentence

## Outputs
- A calm hero-like header strip within the `header` card.
- Plain-language copy that explains the app goal and encourages action confidence.

## State
- Stateless.
- No form state or side effects.

## Dependencies
- `components/HeaderStrip.tsx`
- Global token styles in `app/globals.css`

## Edge cases
- Copy must avoid jargon.
- One-line purpose must be short enough to scan quickly.
- No em dashes in copy.

## Acceptance checks
- The header explains the product in one screenful under ten seconds.
- First line hierarchy is clear: title, purpose, reassurance.
- Clean spacing and no decorative background art or clutter.
